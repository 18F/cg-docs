Try cloud.gov for free

Follow this quick start guide to create an account and deploy a sample app in your own free sandbox.

Get trial access and a free sandbox account

Federal employees and contractors with an email ending in .mil, .gov, or .fed.us can obtain a free sandbox account. To register, you’ll need access to your email and a smartphone with Google Authenticator installed.

Go build in your sandbox

Follow any of the tutorials below to launch your own web application from your sandbox. You’ll need your cloud.gov account and some familiarity with Windows Powershell or Mac/Linux terminal.
