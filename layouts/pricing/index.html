Estimating cost

Placeholder text.

All packages are priced based on three components:

  • Access package

    What package you choose — Prototyping ($1250/month), FISMA Low ($1667/month per system), or FISMA Moderate ($7500/month per system) — depends on what kinds of applications you want to host.

  • Resource usage

    The memory, in MB of RAM, you reserve for your applications. Assessed monthly (approximately $100/GB per month) and doesn’t roll over month-to-month.

  • Add-on services

    A la carte services that cloud.gov can spin up quickly and run on your behalf. See the Add-on services () and plans offered for each service by running cf marketplace.
